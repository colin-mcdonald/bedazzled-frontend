About Bedazzled

A Note From Mark & Tracy

Bedazzled 2016 was another incredible season. Thank you to everyone for your positive energy backstage. Having such a friendly calm atmosphere makes everything run so smooth and enjoyable. Seeing you all supporting each other is fantastic. That's what we are all about - a fun, healthy dance experience that you will treasure for years to come. Our focus is "For the Kidz!" in 2016, Bedazzled gave away over $350,000.00 in cash prizes, awards and give aways!

We look forward to sharing this wonderful dance experience with you and your students in 2017! Don't miss out, register early!

Regards,
Mark & Tracy
